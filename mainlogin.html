What makes IWC Watches unique?

IWC Watches are renowned for their precision craftsmanship, innovative engineering, and timeless designs. Each timepiece combines Swiss tradition with modern technology, making it a masterpiece of luxury.

Are IWC Watches water-resistant?

Most IWC watches are water-resistant to varying degrees, depending on the model. Always check the specifications of your timepiece and avoid exposing it to extreme conditions unless designed for such use.
